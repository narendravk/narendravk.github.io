I started my career on construction sites — scheduling metro lines, managing real-estate projects, and wrestling with Primavera P6 spreadsheets. Along the way I discovered that the real bottleneck wasn't the concrete; it was the lack of good software built by people who actually understood construction.

So I taught myself to code. Today I'm Co-Founder & Lead Developer at Revconn Tech, where I build cloud-based project management and monitoring tools specifically tailored to the construction sector — automating workflows that used to eat entire teams' weeks.

I'm a Python developer at heart, comfortable across the full stack: Django & Flask on the backend, JavaScript and React on the front, and Android when I need to put something in a site-manager's pocket. My superpower is translating domain problems — things non-technical stakeholders struggle to articulate — into working software.
