Engage Your Mind: Active Aging Through Play.

Welcome to a collection of engaging brain and memory games designed to support cognitive wellness in senior citizens. Developed in collaboration with my sister, Yaashodaa Kashikar-Padhye, who is pursuing a Ph.D. in this vital area, these tools are built on the principles of active aging and dementia prevention. Our goal is to provide fun, stimulating activities that help keep the mind active, sharp, and resilient!

The Corsi Block-Tapping Test (also known as the Corsi Span Test) is a widely used neuropsychological task designed to measure visuospatial short-term memory and working memory capacity.
